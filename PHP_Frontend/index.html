F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

What type of palm oil do you sell?

We offer premium-quality red palm oil in retail and bulk quantities.Sizes range from 1L to 50L drums, with options for wholesale packaging.

How do I place a booking for a service?

Simply fill out our online Service Booking Form by selecting your desired service, date, time and providing your contact details. You will receive a confirmation shortly after submission.

Do you deliver palm oil to my location?

Yes! We offer delivery services within Ghana for bulk and retail orders. Delivery fees vary based on location and order volume.

Can I visit your farm?

Yes, we welcome farm visits for schools, organizations, and individuals.You can book a tour through our Farm Visit Booking section.

How do I contact your team for enquires?

You can send a message using the Enquiry Form on the Enquiry Booking page. Alternatively, reach us via email or phone on the Contact Us page.

What payment methods do you accept?

Currently, we accept cash on delivery, mobile money (MTN MoMo), and bank transfers. Online payments will be available soon.
